About

"Forsaken Eclipse" is an RPG characterised by its pronounced Dark Fantasy and horror elements. The intensity of the game is heightened by the strong focus on strategic decision-making, group management, turn-based combat and survival mechanics. Furthermore, even when performing seemingly mundance tasks, the game throws outcome changing tarot cards at the player, providing replayability.

Team

The project was developed by myself in collaboration with a single artist. Whilst the artist worked on creating ALL art assets and I worked on implementing ALL mechanics and VFX, we both worked closely together from the game design stage, to the final prototype. The goal of this project was to create a portfolio piece.

Mechanics

Turn-Based Combat System

A port system that has completely seamless transitions. Along with working with the physics system, the system is also optimized, which allows for multiple portals at once.

Character Stats, Equipement & Party

The player has a party of up to 4 characters, where each one has their own skills, items and stats. Once the player selects a character, they are able to verify their details. From there, clicking any item slot will show a list of items that can be equipped to that slot.

Notice how when hovering over an item, the player can preview what their resulting stats will be.
