About

"Forsaken Eclipse" is an indie game project under development in the RPG, Adventure, Strategy and Dark Fantasy genres. The player needs to lead this group of survivors through the fog and dangers of the world, bearing in mind that their choices will influence the relationships between the characters and the course of the game. It also features several tarot cards that will set in motion the fate of the player party.
