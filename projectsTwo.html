GO HOME

Project Overview

A third person puzzled-based sandbox game with adventure and visual novel elements where the player takes on the role of a lost puppy looking for it's owner.

My Role

Being the only programmer, i was responsible for the implementation of all systems, animations, shaders, etc... Furthermore, i was also responsible for all documentation.

Developed key documents(GDD, Business Plan, UML).
Applied multiple design pattern and OOP principles.

Colored Rails

Project Overview

A mobile hyper-casual prototype made in 6 days that aimed to replicate the game Roof Rails. However, it has the added twist of color-based interactions.

My Role

Being the sole developer, i implemented all mechanics, VFX's, post-processing effects and animations (mixamo). I also modelled all assets (except the main character).

Key Points

The player's pole only interacts with objects of the same color.
Works in PC and mobile (although it is designed for mobile).
Use of OOP principles in order to achieve a scalability.
